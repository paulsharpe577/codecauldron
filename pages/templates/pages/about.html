About Code Cauldron Portishead

Welcome to Code Cauldron Portishead!

Are you an adult eager to learn coding and make new friends? Code Cauldron Portishead is the perfect place to concoct your coding skills while connecting with others in a fun, in-person setting!

Bi-weekly, we dive into a new topic, from Python and microcontrollers to web development. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our sessions are designed to help you grow and enjoy the journey.

What to Expect:

  • Bi-weekly sessions covering exciting coding topics
  • Friendly, hands-on learning in a small group (max 8 people)
  • Run by an experienced coder, offering guidance and support
  • A welcoming environment for building friendships and confidence

No matter your skill level, come join us and concoct your passion for coding!
